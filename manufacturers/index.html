Scannable is an end-to-end Platform for Smart PPE™ manufacturing

Scannable's Smart PPE™ platform let's you add serial numbers, data matrix, QR codes or NFC Tags to any product category, with production tools for printing labels, laser etching and NFC tag linking.

Digitise your SKUs with NFC

QR codes are great, but they can get damaged and become unreadable.

Scannable can help you streamline NFC embedding at production. NFC Tags carry the same information but are more durable, letting your customer use them for the full lifespan of the equipment.

A central location to store all your product data

Put your SKU data in one easy-to-access location. Save compliance documents, product specifications, serial and batch numbers, and date of manufacture to a single source of truth.

The first PIM built for PPE

PIM stands for ‘Product Information Management’, and we’ve built the first PIM focused on handling PPE data.

Scannable reflects the reality of your product range. The Scannable PIM is a place to store and share all of your product’s data and documents, and it gives you the tools to solve for metric vs. imperial conversions and handling product lifespan.

Group SKUs into products and variants

Grouping your SKUs allows you to efficiently update data across many SKUs at once.

Share SKU data with Resellers

Give equipment resellers access to accurate product information with Scannable’s API and your brand’s SKU data. It's like sharing the data to your own website, but we make it available to your customer to use within their software of choice.

Serialisation tools to fit your organisation

Centralise your product range’s traceability in one dashboard. Use Scannable to create serial numbers or capture them from your various production locations so you can see what you’ve produced,

Create serials on Scannable

Looking for a modern serial creation process? Scannable has tools to create serials in any format you need, store them on the cloud so you never lose your data, and export a spreadsheet to send to your production line.

Upload serials to Scannable

Capture serials already being created on your production lines by bulk upload, or one by one using the Scannable app to scan existing 2D code serial numbers (like data matrix).

serial creation

OEM / Supplier Logins

Give your OEM / third-party suppliers restricted access to your Scannable account so they can use the platform to perform their production tasks, without editing or viewing your data.

See your products and their usage in the Forwards Traceability Dashboard

Whether you create serial numbers within your ERP or outside it, Scannable connects your serials with the SKU data your customers need, adding value and driving end user registration of your products. This makes it easy to know what you’ve made, whether it’s registered, and how to communicate with users when necessary.

ERP systems are good at managing your upstream supply of materials, and tracking the bill of materials in production, this is known as ‘backwards traceability’.

Forwards Traceability
