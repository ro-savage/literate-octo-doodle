Retrofitted solutions are dead. Long live Smart PPE™

Smart PPE™, powered by Scannable, is equipment with a digital ID or passport, holding all their critical information, movements and inspection history, available at the tap of a phone.

Using Smart PPE™ is the easiest way to cut out data entry
Step 01
Scan the NFC tag or QR
code on your Smart PPE™.
Smart PPE™ comes loaded with the serial, batch and part numbers, date of manufacture, expiry date, plus all of the product’s data and documents.
Step 02
View the product with its information already loaded.
The item’s unique information and product data is already entered in Scannable, so you don’t need to type a thing!
Step 03
Add the item to
your inventory.
Easy! You can now start to manage your item, record inspection results, and more.
