About

Highnovate is developing unique and innovative solutions for technical problems in the field of working at heights and in enabling access to far-reach points.

The company’s focus is on customers who use working at heights for industrial or tactical purposes such as rescue missions and law enforcement operations.

Highnovate has unique capabilities and more than 30 years of field and manufacturing experience, enabling them to respond to their customers' needs quickly, and develop new creative solutions for their problems.
