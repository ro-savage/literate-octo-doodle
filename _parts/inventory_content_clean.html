FAQs

What is the best equipment tracking system with compliance reporting?

The best systems combine equipment tracking software with built-in compliance management tools. Scannable is designed specifically for safety equipment, allowing teams to track gear inventories, allocate equipment to jobs, and record inspections with instant audit trails — all in one platform.

How do I track inspections to comply with regulations?

To comply with LOLER (UK), OSHA (US), or WHS (AU), inspection records must be documented, retained, and easily retrievable. A compliance tracking platform like Scannable stores inspection results digitally, links them directly to gear, and makes records searchable in seconds for regulators and audits.

What’s the alternative to spreadsheets for compliance inspections?

Spreadsheets are manual, error-prone, and hard to scale. A cloud-based compliance and inventory management platform automates compliance workflows, sends reminders for inspections, and creates audit-ready records without manual data entry.

How does compliance software create audit trails?

Every inspection, allocation, or status update is logged automatically. A digital audit trail provides proof of compliance for regulators and auditors, ensuring full accountability and transparency.
